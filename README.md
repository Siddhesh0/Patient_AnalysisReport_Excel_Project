🏥 Patient Analysis Report – Excel Project
This project focuses on analyzing a large healthcare dataset containing patient demographics, medical details, insurance information, and administrative records. The dataset includes Patient IDs, Age, Gender, Health Metrics, Medical History, Insurance Data, Admission/Discharge Dates, and Treatment Costs.

🔑 Key Features
Data Cleaning & Preparation: Structured raw patient records for analysis.

Demographic Insights:

Age group and gender distribution.

Regional and hospital-level patient statistics.

Medical & Insurance Analysis:

Treatment cost breakdowns.

Insurance coverage vs. out-of-pocket expenses.

Operational Metrics:

Admission and discharge trends.

Patient turnover and length of stay analysis.

Risk & Outcome Tracking: Identified patterns in patient outcomes and healthcare utilization.

📈 Potential Visualizations
Pie charts for gender and age group distribution.

Bar charts for insurance coverage vs. medical expenses.

KPI cards for average treatment cost, patient turnover, and length of stay.

Line charts for admission/discharge trends over time.

Dashboards combining demographics, costs, and outcomes.

🎯 Purpose
This project demonstrates Excel-based healthcare analytics and reporting skills, showcasing capabilities in data cleaning, patient demographics analysis, insurance insights, and hospital performance tracking. It can be extended into Power BI for interactive healthcare dashboards.
